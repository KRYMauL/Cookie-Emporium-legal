Security

Cookie Emporium is built on a local-first storage model. All gameplay data — including save progress, achievements, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage mechanisms provided by your browser or operating system. This data is never transmitted to, or stored on, any server operated by the Service Provider, because no such server exists.

Because gameplay data never leaves your device, it cannot be intercepted in transit, accessed in a remote data breach, or exposed through a compromise of infrastructure the Service Provider does not operate.
